# Goal

Status: active

## Active Mission

Turn all 881,069 RAWG records into an exhaustive, evidence-backed, pointer-driven game decomposition and one continuously tracked master inventory. Every game must preserve all direct evidence, category signals, platform constraints, rare mechanics, effects, interactions, timing, conditions, and reusable kit boundaries. Build and runtime-prove every genuinely novel master kit accepted by the authoritative Codex and inventory gates; candidates and proposals never count as built kits.

## Active Completion Criteria

- Exactly 881,069 unique source identities reconcile with evidence maps and compact game-kit pointer maps; malformed, skipped, duplicated, or untraceable rows are zero.
- Every evidence-bearing game is decomposed through versioned facet profiles into all supported atomic mechanic, lifecycle, state, proof, adapter, and composition boundaries. Sparse games remain explicitly evidence-limited rather than padded.
- The master inventory preserves rare one-source capabilities, exact pointer queries, support counts, Engine/ProtoKits comparison, and Git/dirty-tree/AST processing epochs.
- LFM 350M runs at up to 64 concurrent evidence-extraction lanes; LFM 1.2B runs at up to eight skeptical refinement lanes. Rejections of protected evidence fail forward into Codex review instead of disappearing.
- Codex reviews aggregated master candidates, can repair grounded contracts, rejects unsupported or duplicate boundaries, and never treats benchmark builds as integrated capabilities.
- Every accepted novel master has a generated ESM kit, immutable build ledger, and passing NexusSimulator `kit.runtime-proof`. Thousands of runtime-proven kits—not rows, clusters, candidates, or requests—are required when the corpus supports them.
- No source or derived evidence is silently rewritten. Processing holds below 10 GiB free, resumes from durable pointers, and promotion outside KitUniverse remains approval-gated.
